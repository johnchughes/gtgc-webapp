John

After taking a hiatus in the last 2000's he started collecting 40k again around 2013 starting with Chaos Space Marines and later Tau. As well as 40k he also plays boardagmes, Plays/DM's dungeons and dragons, dark heresy, or any RPG system that takes his fancy.

John started going down the GTGC mid 2019, and has not won a game of 40k since.

Luke

Luke started his tabletop gaming back in the late 90s. His first taste of Battle was in the form of the magazine “lord of the rings battles in middle earth” which was a bi weekly subscription which gave out a metal character in each issue. This sparked his interest in other GW games and he bought his first 40k starter set “Battle for Makragg” that he split with his friend.

Luke took a long 10 year break shortly after that and didn’t come back too the hobby until the introduction of 40ks 8th edition. Luke has been helping the club for the past year by founding and producing the podcast. You can find Luke at the GTGC bar with a guest Ale and a few packets on O!nks.
