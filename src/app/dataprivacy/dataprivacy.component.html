Privacy & Cookie Policy

Data Privacy

The Gloucester Tabletop Gaming Community (GTGC) will not share any personal information collected about you with third parties. All data is for internal use only and so admins of the GTGC can contact you regarding your account.

Cookie Policy

The Gloucester Tabletop Gaming Community (GTGC) uses cookie trackers for google analytics, no personal information is stored and any information gathered is for GTGC use only. All data collected is anonymised by the analytics platform and individual users can not be identified.
